XRPASCEND Logo Kit

Files
- logo_xrpascend_mark.svg
  Best for profile icons, wallet icons, and token list thumbnails.
- logo_xrpascend_badge.svg
  Best for announcements, campaign banners, and community graphics.
- logo_xrpascend_wordmark.svg
  Best for website hero sections and X header/banner usage.

Color Palette
- Midnight: #0B1020
- Royal Blue: #1A2C64
- Electric Blue: #60A5FA
- Cyan Accent: #67E8F9
- Violet Accent: #C4B5FD
- Light Text: #E2E8F0

Design Intent
- Upward arrow integrated with an 'A' shape communicates growth ("ascend").
- Circular forms keep it token/coin-native.
- Blue-violet gradients tie to XRPL/fintech visual language.

Quick Usage Rules
- Keep clear space around logo equal to at least 15% of logo width.
- Do not stretch or rotate the logo.
- Prefer dark backgrounds for maximum contrast.
- For small avatars, use the mark only (logo_xrpascend_mark.svg).
